Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is one of Indiaâ€™s most popular paneer gravy recipes,
and with good reason! Indian cottage cheese cubes are smothered in a creamy, lightly spiced tomato sauce that is downright delicious.

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Heat oil in a pan. Add in onions and saute for 2 mins.
  • Add in cashews and mix well. Add in tomatoes and cook till mushy. take this in a blender and puree till smooth.
  • Heat 1 tbsp butter and Add in cumin seeds, ginger garlic paste and let them sizzle.
  • Add in pureed onion tomato masala and saute till oil separates.
  • Add in spice powders, salt, sugar and mix well.
  • Add in paneer and water and bring it to a boil. Simmer for 5 mins.
  • Add in fresh cream, kasuri methi leaves and coriander leaves. Mix well.
  • Add in 1 tbsp butter and mix well and Serve.

Ingredients

Ingredients Measurements
Paneer 200 grams
Onion 1
Tomato 4
Cashew 10
Ginger Garlic Paste 1 tbsp
Kashmiri Chilli Powder 1 tbsp
Garam Masala 1 tbsp
